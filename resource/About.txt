The Whisper plug-in analyzes the text present in the audio stream and generates markers corresponding to the tokens (words and/or syllables) found.

The Whisper plug-in is an implementation of the Whisper speech recognition model developed by OpenAI  as a Vamp plug-in. 

- Whisper Vamp plug-in by Pierre Guillot at Ircam (https://www.ircam.fr/)
- Whisper.cpp by Georgi Gerganov (https://ggerganov.com/)
- Whisper model by OpenAI (https://openai.com/)
- Vamp by the Centre for Digital Music, Queen Mary, University of London (www.vamp-plugins.org)
